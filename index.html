Who am I?

I am Coach Tom - former cycling competitor with over 15 years of combined racing and coaching experience. I offer training plans, performance analysis, mental coaching health consultations and pre-hab guidance (stretching, conditioning etc.) for cyclist of various levels, from newcomers to seasoned racers. I am a registered Czech Cycling Federation Level 3 Coach.

What are the benefits of having a cycling coach?

A good coach can be very helpful when it comes to developing a training plan, improving your cycling skills and dealing with problems such as performance plateaus. Objectivity is another benefit of coaching. A coach can objectively diagnose problems that are impacting your performance.
